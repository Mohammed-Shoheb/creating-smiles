➤The purpose of the trust is to support and enhance the welfare and development of orphans by providing education, healthcare, shelter, and other necessary support.

➤You are eligible to make a donation if you have attained the age of majority i.e. eighteen (18) years or you are a company/proprietorship or any other entity duly registered/incorporated under the laws of India.

➤Beneficiaries of the trust are orphans or children from low-income families in need of support for education, healthcare, or shelter.

➤Beneficiaries are selected based on criteria established by the trustees, including need, potential for development, and adherence to the trust’s objectives.

➤The trust shall maintain accurate financial records and accounts of all transactions.

➤An annual report shall be prepared and made available to the public, detailing the trust’s activities, financial status, and accomplishments.

➤Remaining assets go to similar charitable organizations.
